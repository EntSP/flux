* Symptoms of neglect

---

* Control panel
* Check that all buttons on the control panel function.
* Pressing a button does not affect the robot.

---

* Emergency stop
* Check that all Emergency stop buttons work. Push down the Emergency stop button, and check that the Restart button lights up and that the status lights turn red.

    Verify that an emergency stop message is displayed in the robot interface.

* Pressing a button does not bring the robot into Emergency stop.

---

* Manual stop
* To check that the Stop button works, push the button and check that the robot stops and that the Stop button blinks red.
* Pressing the Stop button does not bring the robot into Manual stop.

---

* Restart button
* To check that the Restart button works, first enable Manual stop by pressing the Stop button. Press the Restart button and verify that this brings the robot out of Manual stop.
* Pressing the Restart button does not bring the robot out of Protective or Manual stop.
