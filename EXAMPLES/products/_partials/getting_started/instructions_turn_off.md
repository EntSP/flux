To turn off the robot, follow these steps:

1. Ensure that the robot is not moving or executing an action.
2. Press the Power button for three seconds.

    {% image src=$image_power_button alt="The location of the Power button on the control panel."  /%}

3. Wait for the robot to finish the shutdown process. The status lights waver orange, and the Power button blinks red. All lights turn off when the robot is completely shut down.
