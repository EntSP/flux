# Clean the  exterior and verify attachment of covers {% tag="clean_the_exterior_and_verify_attachment_of_covers" %}

<image: "mir250\_maintenance\_covers.png">

## Purpose

Limiting the amount of dust particles and other debris that can enter the internal parts of the robot. You should also inspect the general condition of the compartment covers.

Frequency: **weekly**

To complete this task, the robot does not need to be powered.

Turn off the robot, and disconnect the battery.

  * When performing maintenance on robots, you risk electrical shock from electrical wires and connectors if internal components are exposed, crushing, collision, or pinching from unexpected movements.

## Procedure

  1. Inspect and verify that all compartment covers are attached correctly.

  2. Inspect and verify that the compartment covers are not damaged.

  3. Wipe down the compartment covers with a damp cloth. Pay particular attention to the horizontal surfaces of the compartment covers as most dust and debris will collect there.

Do not touch the safety laser scanners with the damp cloth as you risk leaving streaks that can affect scanner performance.

Do not use compressed air to clean the robot exterior.

See a [video](https://vimeo.com/896166190) of the process on the Mobile Industrial Robots profile on vimeo.com.

## Pass criteria <image: "mmg\_pass.svg">

  * All compartment covers are attached correctly.

  * No compartment covers are damaged.

  * Dust and debris is removed from the compartment covers.
