# Inspect the antennas {% tag="inspect_the_antennas" %}

<image: "mir250\_maintenance\_antennas.png">

## Purpose

To ensure optimum wireless communication performance.

Top modules will affect the positions of antennas on MiR250 since they will almost always be relocated to somewhere else as part of building the final robot application, but the principles of a solid connection and proper placement remains the same.

Frequency: **weekly**

To complete this task, the robot does not need to be powered.

Turn off the robot, and disconnect the battery.

  * When performing maintenance on robots, you risk electrical shock from electrical wires and connectors if internal components are exposed, crushing, collision, or pinching from unexpected movements.

## Procedure

  1. Verify that the antennas are properly attached in their sockets.

  2. Verify that the antennas are angled perpendicular to the wireless access points throughout the site.

## Pass criteria <image: "mmg\_pass.svg">

  * All antennas are properly attached in their sockets.

  * All antennas are angled perpendicular to wireless access points.

  * No antennas are clearly damaged or bent.
