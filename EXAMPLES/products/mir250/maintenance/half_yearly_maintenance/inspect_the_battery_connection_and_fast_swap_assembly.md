# Inspect the battery connection and fast-swap assembly {% tag="inspect_the_battery_connection_and_fast_swap_assembly" %}

<image: "mir250\_maintenance\_inspect\_battery\_connection.png">

## Purpose

To ensure that a reliable battery connection can be made, and that the battery locking mechanism works as intended.

Frequency: **half-yearly**

To complete this task, the robot does not need to be powered.

Turn off the robot.

  * When performing maintenance on robots, you risk electrical shock from electrical wires and connectors if internal components are exposed, crushing, collision, or pinching from unexpected movements.

## Procedure

  1. With the battery connected, attempt to pull the Battery lever down. The lever should not be able to move without pulling the Battery lock pin outwards.

  2. Pull the Battery lock pin outwards in order to unlock the Battery lever and confirm that the lever can be pulled down. See [Disconnecting the battery](../How to disconnect the battery.htm).

  3. Verify that the battery fast-swap assembly moves down with the Battery handle, making it possible to remove the battery, once it is fully disconnected. See [Inspect and clean the battery](hym_inspect_and_clean_battery.htm).

See a [video](https://vimeo.com/896160912) of the process on the Mobile Industrial Robots profile on vimeo.com.

## Pass criteria <image: "mmg\_pass.svg">

  * The Battery lever works as intended and can move up and down smoothly once it is unlocked using the Battery lock pin.

  * The Battery lever cannot move when it is locked by the Battery lock pin.

  * The battery fast-swap assembly moves up and down with the Battery lever.

## Remediation

{% table %}

 * Issue
 * Remedial action
 ---
 * The Battery lever cannot move up and down correctly, or the battery pin is unable to lock the Battery lever in place.
 * Replace the Battery lever assembly. See the guide How to replace the battery connector handle on MiR250. You can find this guide on [MiR Support Portal](https://supportportal.mobile-industrial-robots.com/documentation/how-to-replace/how-to-replace-the-battery-connector-handle-on-mir250/).
