# Check safety markings in the environment {% tag="check_safety_markings_in_the_environment" %}

<image: "safety\_markings\_environment.png">

## Purpose

To verify that safety markings are intact and are clearly visible in the operating environment, so that, for example, operating hazard zones can always be clearly identified by personnel.

All operating hazard zones should be clearly marked according to safety standards EN 1525 and ISO 3691-4. See MiR Commissioning Guide or _MiR Fleet Enterprise documentation_ for more information. You can find this guide on [MiR Support Portal.](https://supportportal.mobile-industrial-robots.com/)

Frequency: **quarterly**

## Procedure

  1. Visually inspect all safety markings around robot operational areas, in particular operating hazard zones.

  2. Verify that all safety markings are clearly visible and not obscured by items or installations.

  3. Verify that the markings are intact, not worn down, and that they are clear and easy to discern in the environment.

## Pass criteria <image: "mmg\_pass.svg">

  * All safety markings are intact and not obstructed by items.

  * All appropriate safety markings are in place.
