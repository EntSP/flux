# Check the drive wheel shaft nuts {% tag="check_the_drive_wheel_shaft_nuts" %}

<image: "mir250\_inspect\_drive\_wheel\_shaft\_nuts\_intro.png">

## Purpose

To ensure that the drive wheel shaft nuts are still aligned relative to the center shaft. Any nuts that are moving and coming loose over time can be discovered before causing issues.

Frequency: **half-yearly**

To complete this task, the robot does not need to be powered.

Turn off the robot, and disconnect the battery.

  * When performing maintenance on robots, you risk electrical shock from electrical wires and connectors if internal components are exposed, crushing, collision, or pinching from unexpected movements.

## Procedure

  1. Remove the front and rear compartment covers.

     1. Remove the two screws in the front cover, and remove the cover from the robot. Use a T30 bit.

<image: "front\_unscrew.png"> <image: "front\_remove.png">

     2. Push the two buttons on the rear cover, and remove the cover from the robot.

<image: "rear\_press\_buttons.png"> <image: "rear\_remove.png">

  2. Remove the side compartment covers.

     1. Turn the two screws counterclockwise on the side cover, and remove the cover from the robot. Use a T30 bit.

<image: "side\_unscrew.png">

  3. Visually inspect the drive wheel shaft nut, and verify that the marking from production still lines up. The marking must line up from start to finish.

<image: "mir250\_inspect\_drive\_wheel\_shaft\_nuts\_2.png">

  4. Verify that the lock washer has a tab bend down into one of the grooves in the drive wheel shaft nut.

## Pass criteria <image: "mmg\_pass.svg">

  * None of the drive wheel shaft nuts or lock washers are misaligned or loose.

## Remediation

{% table %}

 * Issue
 * Remedial action
 ---
 * The drive wheel shaft nut or lock washer is damaged and cannot be retightened.
 * Replace the drive wheel. See the guide How to replace the drive wheels on MiR250. You can find this guide on [MiR Support Portal](https://supportportal.mobile-industrial-robots.com/documentation/how-to-replace/how-to-replace-the-drive-wheels-on-mir250/).
