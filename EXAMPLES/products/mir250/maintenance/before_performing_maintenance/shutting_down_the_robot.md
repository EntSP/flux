# Shutting down the robot {% tag="shutting_down_the_robot" %}

Follow this procedure to shut down the robot:

  1. Ensure that the robot is not moving or executing an action.

  2. Press the Power button for three seconds.

<image: "control\_panel\_running\_turn-off.png">

  3. Wait for the robot to finish the shutdown process. The status lights waver orange, and the Power button blinks red.

<image: "control\_panel\_shutting\_down.png">

When the robot finishes the shutdown process, the status and signal lights go off.

<image: "control\_panel\_off.png">
