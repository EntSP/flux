# Things you will need {% tag="things_you_will_need" %}

This section lists which items are needed for different tasks and what to prepare before performing maintenance on the robot.

Lifting the robot

---

A lifting device, capable of lifting at least 400 kg
Four M8 × 16 mm eye-bolts
A sturdy work bench or equivalent table or supports to hold the robot if performing the maintenance while the robot is lifted off of the floor
Two wooden blocks or similar for supporting the robot
Cleaning tasks

---

A couple of microfiber cloths
A couple of regular cloths
Clean tap water for general cleaning purposes
Cotton swabs for cleaning the proximity sensors
Maintenance of charging pads

---

A small mirror to inspect the charging pads
Acetic acid for cleaning the charging pads

  * Alternatively, grit 3 000 sand paper or finer
  * Alternatively, a 1:1 ratio solution of citric acid or household vinegar and table salt (NaCl)

Deionized water for rinsing the charging pads
Other items

---

Nitrile gloves for handling chemicals and checking for oil leaks
A flashlight to inspect 3D cameras and safety laser scanner optics covers
Tape measure for performing the standard brake test
Tape measure or vernier caliper for measuring drive wheel and caster wheel wear
A 10 mm hex wrench and a 10 mm hex wrench for enabling fast swap (if not already enabled) A torque wrench or screwdriver ranging from 7.5 to 15 Nm with the following interchangeable bits:

  * A T20 for removing the status light bracket
  * A T30 for removing the front and side covers
  * A T45 for removing the mounting screws in the top plate
