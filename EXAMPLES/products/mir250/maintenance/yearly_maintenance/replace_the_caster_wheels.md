# Replace the caster wheels {% tag="replace_the_caster_wheels" %}

<image: "mir250\_maintenance\_caster\_wheels.png">

## Purpose

The caster wheels must be replaced yearly, regardless of wear. Depending on floor conditions, usage, and payloads, the caster wheels can develop drag over time, resulting in increased rolling resistance and skidding issues.

Frequency: **yearly**

To complete this task, the robot does not need to be powered.

Turn off the robot, and disconnect the battery.

  * When performing maintenance on robots, you risk electrical shock from electrical wires and connectors if internal components are exposed, crushing, collision, or pinching from unexpected movements.

## Procedure

Follow the procedure as described in How to replace the caster wheels on MiR250. You can find this guide on [MiR Support Portal](https://supportportal.mobile-industrial-robots.com/).

## Pass criteria <image: "mmg\_pass.svg">

  * All four caster wheels have been replaced as prescribed.
