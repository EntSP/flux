# Warning labels

The robot is supplied with the following warning labels in the following locations:

* On the tiller head to warn that Protective fields are muted when driving the robot with the tiller.

    {% image src="/images/mir1200_pallet_jack/safety_stickers/muted_fields_sticker.webp" alt="Warning label - Protective fields muted when tiller in drive position" /%}

* On the frame on top of the hydraulic tank to warn that there is a hydraulic pressure system.

    {% image src="/images/mir1200_pallet_jack/safety_stickers/hydraulic_sticker.webp" alt="Warning label - Hydraulic pressure system warning" /%}

* On both sides of the mast to warn not to stand on or under the forks.

    {% image src="/images/mir1200_pallet_jack/safety_stickers/stand_on_forks_sticker.webp" alt="Warning label - Do not stand on or under forks" /%}

* On top of and on the front of the front compartment under the top cover to warn of risk of crushing hand and hot surface area.

    {% image src="/images/mir1200_pallet_jack/safety_stickers/hand_crush_sticker.webp" alt="Warning label - Risk of crushing hand and hot surface area" /%}

* On the inside of the power interface hatch to describe how and when to use the Main battery disconnect switch.

    {% image src="/images/mir1200_pallet_jack/safety_stickers/main_disconnect_sticker.webp" alt="Warning label - Main battery disconnect switch instructions" /%}

* On the mast behind the right rear-cover to warn that failing to follow the correct battery disconnect sequence may cause system damage and electrical shock.

    {% image src="/images/mir1200_pallet_jack/safety_stickers/batteries_sticker.webp" alt="Warning label - Battery disconnect sequence warning" /%}
