## Lift the robot

To lift the robot:

1. Remove the four M8 mounting bolts in the top plate of the robot. Use a T45 bit. Tighten the bolts to 15 Nm when remounting them.

    {% image src="/images/mir250/move_transportation/remove_top_mounting_screws.webp" alt="Remove mounting screws" /%}

2. Mount four M8 × 16 mm eye bolts to the mounting holes. Screw the eye bolts all the way in. Do not tighten the bolts more than 7.5 Nm. Orient the eye bolts in parallel to the opposite diagonal bolt to ensure stable lifting.

    {% image src="/images/mir250/move_transportation/attach_eye_bolts_top.webp" alt="Attach eye bolts" /%}

3. Use lifting device rated for at least 400 kg to lift the robot.

    {% image src="/images/mir250/move_transportation/lift_with_harness_top.webp" alt="The robot being lifted with lifting cables." /%}
