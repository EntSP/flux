# Disposal {% tag="disposal" %}

According to the European directive 2012/19/EU, Article 2, paragraph 4) d) and 4) e) (WEEE directive), MiR robots are not in scope of the directive.

The robot is therefore not classified as WEEE and can be disposed of in accordance with the applicable local regulations.

Legal obligations apply to the robot's batteries. Disposing used batteries in the household waste is prohibited.

A recycling label indicates that the battery needs to be recycled and not disposed as municipal waste. Contact your distributor to get specific information about their take back service.
