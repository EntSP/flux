* Specification
* Result

* Min. space around L-marker
* 1.95m in front of the marker and 1.0m from the long bar to the side of the marker

---

* Min. space to adj. wall for charger
* 0.6m to the right of charger, 0.35m to the left of charger.

---

* Min. space to adj. wall for L-marker
* 1,3 m from the long bar

---

* Min. space to adj. wall for V-marker

---

* Min. space to adj. wall for VL-marker
* 0.3m to the right of marker and 0.5m to the left of marker. (With docking offset x = -0.55m, y = 0.1m, yaw = 0deg)

* 45 cm

* Operational corridor width

* 155 cm (default), 120 cm (minimized footprint, muted safety)
