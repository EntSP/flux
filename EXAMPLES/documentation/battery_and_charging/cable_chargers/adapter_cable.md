﻿# Adapter cable

1. Turn off the robot, and remove or access the battery from the robotn++see the user guide or integrator manual for your robot application.

2. Connect the adapter cable to the battery.

{% image src="/images/accessories/batteries_chargers/Connect_adapter_battery.jpg" alt="Connect adapter battery" /%}

3. Connect the cable charger you want to use to a power source. Always do this before connecting the cable charger to the robot or battery.

{% image src="/images/accessories/batteries_chargers/Connect_charger_power.jpg" alt="Connect charger power" /%}

4. Check that the LED is green or orange (depending on your chargern++see [Cable chargers](../cable_chargers/cable_chargers.htm)). This indicates that the charger is on and is ready to be connected.

{% image src="/images/accessories/batteries_chargers/Check_light.jpg" alt="Check light" /%}

If the LED is not on, you may damage the battery and robot if you connect the charger.

- Always wait until the LED has turned on in the right color before connecting the charger to the battery or robot.

5. Connect the cable charger to the adapter cable.

{% image src="/images/accessories/batteries_chargers/Connect_charger_battery.jpg" alt="Connect charger battery" /%}
