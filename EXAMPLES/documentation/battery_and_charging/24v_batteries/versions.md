﻿# Battery versions

There are two types of 48V batteries used in MiR robots: one for MiR500 and MiR1000 robots with HW version 2.0, and another for MiR250, MiR600, and MiR1350 robots.

## MiR250, MiR600, and MiR1350

The battery for MiR250, MiR600, and MiR1350 has a downward-facing connector that is compatible with the lever-based battery disconnection assembly. There are two revisions of this battery. The differences are identified below.

## MiR500 and MiR1000 HW 2.0

The 48V battery for MiR500 and MiR1000 has a connector on top of the battery that is compatible with the battery cable used in MiR500 and MiR1000. It has order number 450567.
