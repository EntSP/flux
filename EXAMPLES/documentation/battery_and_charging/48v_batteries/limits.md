﻿# Example

For a better understanding of how the temperature and time affects the current limits, see the graphs in [Figure 8.1](#Graphs). They display data from MiR250 running for 30 hours under the following conditions:

- With a payload of 150 kg

- At a temperature of 22 n++C

- No current draw from the top Power interface

- The robot was charged to 28% from 10% multiple times and once to 100% from 10%
